How It Works

Publish a Menu

Creating a menu is EASY! Create and publish your menu so your customers can purchase your grub. Includes images!

Customers Purchase

Sell your grub from your IndieChef page or embed your IndieChef eCommerce widget on your own website!

Fulfill the Order

Manage the order status and communicate with the customer. IndieChef even handles delivery tracking!

Our goal is to make being an independent chef EASY.

All to often, we see independent chef's being required to use huge platforms to management their order fulfillment processes. These systems are bulky and confusing. Time for something simple!
