What Is IndieChef?

Our Mission: To help independent chefs and cooks grow their businesses by providing easy-to-use online tools that connect chefs with their customers

Publish your menu

Creating a menu is EASY! Simply add menu items so your customers can purchase your grub. Includes images and customizations!

Take Online Orders

Sell your grub from your own IndieChef page. Integrate with social media. Manage bookings. We even handle customer communication!

Fulfill Orders

Allow customers to pickup their orders, deliver orders yourself, or take advantage of contactless courier delivery through Postmates New

Run Your Business

Use the IndieChef dashboard to track your orders, see trends, and run your kitchen smarter!

IndieChef makes it easy so you can keep cooking!

Features

IndieChef Features

Contactless Courier Delivery  New

As part of the IndieChef membership, offer customers an affordable, reliable, contactless, and commission-free courier delivery service courtesy of Postmates! Control your fulfillment calendar, pickup location, and hours of operation. Customers pay the determined delivery charge based on their location and size of the packages. Chefs and customers get notified via text message at every step of the delivery process.

Flexible Online Menu

Sign up and create a menu from your dashboard. Upload images, descriptions and nutritional data to sell your menu items. Set preferences, manage inventory, and share your menu link and QR code. Easy transactions for customers.

Dashboard

Manage your business online from your IndieChef dashboard. Get complete analytics and real time information about your orders. Your all-in-one spot to run your food business.

Bookings & Invoices

Receive booking requests from customers. Send invoices effortlessly. Customers enjoy a simple transaction process, complete with email and SMS notifications at every turn.

Customer Communication

Send your customers messages about their orders. Receive customer messages from your menu page. Get notified about new orders, bookings, invoice payments and when you get paid!

Compliance Resources

From your dashboard, view Cottage laws for your state and stay compliant based on state regulations.

Get Paid Fast

IndieChefs get paid every 24 hours for every order and paid invoice received. We use PayPal to make the transfer process simple. Get a free PayPal account then start your FREE 30-day trial.

The IndieChef Dashboard



The custom IndieChef Dashboard allows you to see real time data and analytics to help run your chef business. As a chef, you can quickly publish or unpublish your menu as well as share your menu link and QR code. See your earnings, and also manage orders, bookings, and invoices. Track customer orders and see top sellers. Even get a breakdown of the month's sales!
